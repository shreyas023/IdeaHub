Insights


#DAY 00

Day #00 - “Trivia”, is a 40 minute Quiz Session hosted on Unstop, with 30 MCQs for each participating team to tackle. With only 80 seconds per question and time ticking away, teams must race to answer. The first 30 teams to finish the trivia advance to the next round.

#DAY 01

Day #01 -“Mindspace”, round involves understanding problem statements which are received from the industry experts, and developing potential solutions, and submitting a detailed solution report, all under the purview of Industry Experts.

#DAY 02

Day#02 - “Launchpad”, starts with creation of a comprehensive presentation of the proposed solution, and initiating the implementation phase. The final presentations are assessed by the Industry Experts for evaluation, as the event wraps.
